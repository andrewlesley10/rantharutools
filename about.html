Our History & Operations

Rantharu Machines and Tools (Private) Limited was established on 27th September 2013 by Mr. Mohamed Samsudeen Sheik Kamurdeen.

With over 10 years of industry experience, the company has grown into a well-recognized importer and distributor of machinery and tools in Sri Lanka.

Based in the Central Province, we focus on delivering high-quality products to businesses across the country. Our operations are strictly wholesale-oriented, ensuring competitive pricing and reliable supply for our clients.

We take pride in our:

  • Experienced and dedicated team
  • Strong supplier network
  • Commitment to quality and customer satisfaction

We also provide warranty support and after-sales service, ensuring long-term value for our customers.

For inquiries, bulk orders, and pricing, feel free to contact us — we are always ready to serve you with the best solutions.
